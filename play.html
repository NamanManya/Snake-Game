RULES FOR THE GAME

  • This game can only be played on a device that has a keyboard attached
    to it.
  • The white coloured object is the Snake and the Red coloured object is
    the food.
  • The number displayed on the top right corner is your score board.
  • You can move the snake left, right, up and down with the help of the
    arrow keys.
  • While playing if the snake touches the wall or if it cuts its tail then
    your score drops to zero.
  • If you press the arrow key opposite to the snake's direction of motion
    then your score drops to zero.
